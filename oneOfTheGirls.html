About

“One of the Girls” is a collaboration between BLACKPINK's JENNIE, Lily-Rose Depp and The Weeknd for the HBO show The Idol, featured on episode 4 “Stars Belong to the World.”

The song was first revealed in The Weeknd's Instagram Live on April 10, 2023, originally titled “One of the Girls (Tough Love).” The track was then previewed at JENNIE's Calvin Klein Capsule collection pop-up event in Seoul, South Korea, on May 10, 2023.
