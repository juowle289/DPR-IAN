Q&A

What is the mean of "Ballroom Extravaganza"?

"Ballroom Extravaganza" by DPR IAN is an emotive song about a relationship that has come to an end. The narrator wishes for the relationship to be "the same again," but realizes it is too late. He describes the moments leading up to and after the breakup, from the moment "the world stopped for you" to the “ballroom extravaganza” that ultimately pulls the narrator in. Even with all of the “king's horsemen and all the king's men,” they could not “put [the relationship] back together again.” The song illustrates the sadness of letting go and coming to terms with a life-altering event.

What did DPR IAN say about "Ballroom Extravaganza"?

This song is about when the world is ending. You're still looking for that perfect moment on this world and who you're dancing with. Whether it could be the opposite person or just the moment where everything comes together. The world's ending but it doesn't even phase you because you're just so honed in on finding this perfect moment on this dance floor.
